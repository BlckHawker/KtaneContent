On the Subject of Symbol Pad

And I thought that Keypad would be the only time that I’d have to relay symbols.

On the module are nine keys containing symbols.

Find each button’s symbol in the grid below corresponding to its position on the keypad.

Overlay those positions into a single 4×4 grid.

Press the buttons in reading order based on the combined grid, but start in the position indicated by the number of ports on the bomb and wrap back to the start when you reach the end.

If there are no ports, start in position 1. If there are more than 16 ports, repeatedly subtract 16 until in the range 1–16.
