Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Green Levers

On the Subject of Green Levers

8 decisions, and any can be your... first?
Wait, what?

This module has 8 small levers on it.

If the first lever is not green, you are looking at a different module.

To disarm the module, put the other 7 levers into the correct positions, then flip the first switch to submit.
Submitting a wrong combination will register a strike.

To determine the correct state for each lever, use the Venn diagram on the next page to determine what to do.

For this module, the first switch on the panel is Switch 0.
