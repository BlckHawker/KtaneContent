On the Subject of Ducks

A group of rubber ducks is, of course, referred to as a rubber band.

The four differently-colored ducks on the module represent a position in the room below. Squeeze any duck to move in the corresponding direction. Move to the pool in the middle, without crashing into the edge of the room, to solve the module. The room below is not necessarily in the same orientation as the room on the module.
