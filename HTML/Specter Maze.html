On the Subject of Specter Maze

You failed the IQ test for The Company, so the Tax Man took you instead.

  • Welcome to Headass Manor.

  • During your tour of Headass Manor, you seemed to have triggered the Curse of Headass, probably due to your pilfering ways.

  • Consequentially, your spirit and body have been separated and you need to link them back up, lest you want to suffer the Curse of Headass forever.

  • Your body and soul are within two different realms of existence, and as a result, the both of you are traversing through two separate layouts of Headass Manor.

  • You can swap whether you are controlling your body or your soul at any time by pressing the swap button. Both your body and soul share one absolute position.

  • Each room within every Headass Manor layout will have a marked indicator in the blueprints listed on the next page, though these markings are old and may have faded over time. As such, markings may be absent.

  • The Curse of Headass was triggered based on some artifact you picked up during your pilfering tour. Placing it back in its original location will lift the Curse of Headass, combining your body and apparation.
    • Due to the reality shifting nature of the Curse of Headass, you will need to do this for both artifacts collected by both the body and the soul.
