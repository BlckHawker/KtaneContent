On the Subject of Yet Another Keypad

We made a drywall list of keyboard buttons we would like to see: PLEASE, THANK YOU, FUCK OFF, DIE, OOPS...MY MISTAKE, DO SOMETHING COOL AND SURPRISE ME.

This module consists of a grid of 5x5 buttons each one with an icon on it. To solve it you need to find a line or column on the module, anywhere in the table and press all the corresponding buttons. Note: the module checks the answer only once you press all the buttons in a line or column, so you can turn on and off buttons however you like without triggering an error.
