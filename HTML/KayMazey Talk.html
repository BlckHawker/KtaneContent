On the Subject of KayMazey Talk

Lost, confused, and dazed; There is no way out of this tricky maze.

Take note of the word shown on the screen. This is your goal in the maze below. Press the screen to start disarming the module. Upon a strike the module will return to this state.

After the screen is pressed, the word represents your current position. Use the arrows to navigate openings in the corresponding direction.

If the screen does not contain a capital "K", press the opposite arrow instead.
