Original Manual

Assume the symbol that caused the LED to light up to be the top-left corner of these small tables provided on the next page.

Pair each of the same letters together to get the result. Determine which combination of the two offsets result in APPLE-PEN, in that specific order, from the last pair obtained, and nothing else on the other pairs.

Pairs are formed by combining uppercase letters with lowercase letters in the same alphabet. (I.E. Aa, NOT AB)

These offset combinations will NEVER WORK (Treated as X, Y):

  • 2, 2
  • 2, 4
  • 3, 3
  • 4, 2
  • 4, 4
