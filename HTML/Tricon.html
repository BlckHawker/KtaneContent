On the Subject of Tricon

Triple Blan Blast!

Three module icons present on the Repository of Manual Pages have been overlapped and have had an XOR operation applied to their combined RGB color values.

Determine which three module icons are present. Use the up and down arrows to scroll through a list of module names. Use the square button between the arrows to submit that module name.

Submitting an incorrect module will incur a strike.

Submitting a module correctly will remove it from the XOR operation.

Submitting all three modules correctly will disarm the module.
