On the Subject of UFO Satellites

“Maybe this one doesn’t spy on us...”

On the module are six (6) orange kite-shaped satellites that continuously rotate, each one with a number (0-9) on it.
To solve this module, press three satellites so the pattern they create is the correct one. The exact satellites pressed, and the order they are pressed in, do not matter. Only their relative positions do.

Take all indicators on the bomb, order them alphabetically, and concatenate them to form a long word.
Append “UFO” to the end of the word if a Parallel Port is present, or to the beginning otherwise.
Example: Lit IND, unlit SND and lit CAR with a Parallel Port present gives “CARINDSNDUFO”.

From this concatenated word, read the Nth letter where N is the sum of all six digits present on the satellites.
The first letter is considered 1. If you reach the end of the word, loop back around to the first letter and keep counting.
