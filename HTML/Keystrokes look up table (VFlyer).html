On the Look Up of Keystrokes

Perhaps some of the keys that were typed in were not that used at all.

Original Manual

Determine the item with the incorrect colorings and select 1-5 keys that are incorrectly colored to spell that item on QWERTY keyboard. Items spelled with have their letters red on the keyboard.

Press the space bar on the module to cycle from a total of 5 possible unique items. 3 items are associated with the first category, 1 on the second, 1 on the third. The correct item with the incorrect colorings belongs to the third category with the categories abiding by these rules:

  • If the two other categories are in the same row or column, the third category is in that row or column.
  • Otherwise, if the two other categories are not in the same row or column, the third category is in the row or column not shared by the other two.

Selecting the wrong letters on the correct item, or typing on the incorrect item will NOT reset inputs.
