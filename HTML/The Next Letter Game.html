On the Subject of The Next Letter Game

You?!?! V.

  • This needy will have a black screen initially displaying “--” and a button near the top left that has a label saying “-”.
  • Every 50-70 seconds, the needy will activate a 10 second timer.
  • When it activates, the display to change to a letter and a question mark, and the top left button’s label will change to an exclamation mark.
  • When this happens, you must input the next letter of the alphabet (wrapping around if necessary).
  • To input a letter, focus the needy and press the corresponding letter key on your keyboard.
  • This letter can be changed if you try inputing a different one.
  • The inputted letter will replace the question mark on the screen.
  • Press the top left button to submit.
  • The needy will also automatically submit the letter inputted when the 10 second timer runs out.
  • If the input is incorrect (or still a “?”), the needy will incur a strike.
  • Regardless of your answer, the needy will deactivate when time runs out or when the top left button is pressed.
  • The timer is extended to 30 seconds on Twitch Plays.
