Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Annoying Puzzle

On the Subject of Annoying Puzzle

Sounds easy, yet still pretty annoying.

  • The module has 25 buttons, with numbers for range 0-249.
  • Press the buttons in ascending order.
  • There will be 2 stages. First stage will have usual numbers. Second stage numbers’ digits will be replaced by corresponding symbols:
1234567890
!@#$%^&*()
  • After completing two stages, module will be solved.
  • After pressing wrong button a strike will be given and current stage will reset.
