On the Subject of Encoloryption

You can now read colors.

This module consists of 20 buttons that are colored on a dark green background.

Using the table of letter conversions below, translate each colors to their respective letter, following the reading order of the buttons. This will form a message without any spaces. At the end of the message, some uncolored buttons can appear. They may safely be ignored.

The deciphered message will give a mathematical expression. Evaluate the given calculations. If the final number is not in the range 1 to 20, add or subtract 20 until it is.

Lastly, press the button matching your final answer. The buttons are associated with the number of their positions in reading order. (#1 is in the top-left, #2 is right beside it, etc.)

Notes:

  • Anything referred in the serial number only takes in the digits, not the letters.
  • When asked to count in reverse order or from right to left, use the inverse of the reading order. That means that #1 is at the bottom-right and counting continues to the left.
  • For the division, ignore any remainders.
  • “Holders” refer to battery holders. “Plates” refer to port plates.

Appendix EncCBM - Colorblind Mode

In colorblind mode, some symbols are added at the top-left of each buttons. Each symbol is associated with a letter and represent the same letter as the color on the button.
