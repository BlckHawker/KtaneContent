Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Type Beat

On the Subject of Type Beat

No producer tags included.

Flip the switch to activate the module.

An eight-letter word will be revealed one letter at a time. Each display will flash the letter in its position within the word, with the letters appearing in a random order.

Once all eight letters have been flashed, the displays will flash question marks, one at a time, in a different random order. Type the letters of the word in the order indicated by the question mark flashes.

An incorrect input will incur a strike and reset the module.

Entering all eight letters correctly will disarm the module.
