Appendix SWITCH: Identifying lookalikes of Colored Switches

If there are no lit LEDs:
Colored Switches
If the sockets are colored and there is a middle LED between the top and bottom row of LEDs:
Multicolored Switches
If the switches start all down and the bottom row of LEDs are lit up white:
Not Colored Switches
If the first LED is lit:
Recolored Switches
If all of the switches are black:
Uncolored Switches
