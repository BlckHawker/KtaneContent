Submission

Once you have determined the correctly-shifted trait and Roman equivalent, instruct the defuser to select those options on the displays.

Since Chronos wanted this module to have even a small resemblance of time attached to it, he’s made a timed-submission feature.

To submit, determine first how many seconds the defuser will have to submit based on these edgework rulesets:

  • If the bomb contains an serial port OR the serial number contains any of the letters in ‘JDGE’, the defuser will have 2 seconds to submit.
  • Otherwise, if the bomb contains an PS2 port OR the serial number contains any of the letters in ‘GODS’, the defuser will have 3 seconds to submit.
  • Otherwise, the defuser will have 5 seconds to submit.

Read the next paragraph VERY carefully & in full before performing.

To submit, press the stopwatch in the top-left. This will give the defuser how ever many seconds to submit. Then, tell them to press the SUBMIT button. Failure to submit within the time-frame will result in a strike.

You also run the risk of layering a strike if you submit the wrong answer. Make sure your work is correct!
