On the Subject of Pigpen Cycles

Does this even count as a pigpen cipher anymore?

To encrypt:

For each letter:

  1. Look up its position in one of the 3x3 tables below. If your letter appears multiple times, pick any of them.
  2. Move clockwise around the outer edge of the 3x3 grid as many times as there are 45-degree rotations of the corresponding dial. If your letter is E or N, do nothing.
  3. If you moved an odd number of steps (aka odd number of 45-degree rotations), move to the same position of the other 3x3 table vertically in the big 2x2 grid. This also applies if your letter is E or N.

To decrypt:

Move counter-clockwise instead of clockwise.
