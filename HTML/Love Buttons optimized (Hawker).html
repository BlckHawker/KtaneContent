Find the display and press “Yes” if the condition is true. Otherwise press “No”

Display Text Condition
“Do you love me?” # of batteries is even
“Do you want to watch a movie?” # of modules is odd
“Do you want to hang out?” The sum of the digits in the serial number ≥ 8
“Do you need some water?” Serial number does not contain a vowel
“Do you want to kiss?” # of strikes is odd
“Do you want to walk?” # of ports is odd
“Are you ready for the luncheon?” # of indicators ≥ 3
“Do you have any plushies?” Present indicators can spell “SALT” or “CHAV”
“Are you also cold?” # of lit indicators ≥ 2
