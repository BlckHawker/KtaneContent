Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Death Note

On the Subject of Note of Death

"All keikaku according to plan!" - Dark Yagami

In the top right corner are two buttons, the left one is used to call Kira, the other plays todays news. Every day do the following:

If it is currently day 4-6 call Kira to learn what you should do today. Do not call Kira more than once on any given day! Otherwise,

  • Listen to the news. Take note of which criminals have committed crimes today, as well as the types of crimes they have committed (major/petty). You can listen to the news as many times as you want.
  • Find the criminals in the table below for the given day and type of crime.
  • Delete every criminal with an entry in the corresponding cells with the given time and cause of death constraints.

(Note that any criminals with a time of death constraint before the current time on the module cannot be deleted.)

To delete criminals, open the Death Note by clicking on it. For every criminal you have to delete, type their name, time of death and cause of death in the format [FULL NAME] [HOURS:MINUTES:SECONDS] [CAUSE OF DEATH]. If multiple criminals need to be deleted, use the ⌄ button or press the enter key to move to the next line. Note that while you can delete what you have typed in the current line, you cannot delete anything on previous lines. Note further that you should not advance one more line after you have finished writing the last line. Make sure each line is formatted exactly as mentioned or the module will strike. Once you finished writing in the Death Note, close it by clicking the top left corner and press the appropriate button to advance to the next day.

The correct button to advance to the next day is "GO ON" unless stated otherwise by Kira. This means the module cannot solve before day 4, additionally there cannot be more than 6 days. Kira will either instruct you to PASS ON the Death Note or RETURN it to Kira, at which point the module will solve.

Make sure not to let the module time go past 23:59:59 as it will strike unconditionally when this happens. Each stage can only generate with a module time of 23:30:00 at the latest, so you get at least 30 minutes real time per stage.

On a strike the module regenerates the current day, including the news as well as Kira’s instructions.
