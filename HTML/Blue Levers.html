On the Subject of Blue Levers

8 decisions, only one matters.
Wait, no, that's too short.

This module has 8 small levers on it.
If the first lever is not blue, you are looking at a different module.

To disarm the module, figure out which lever is safe, then flip it.

Step 1 : Safe Lever Candidates

Use the colour of the second and third lever to figure out the first candidate, fourth and fifth for the second, sixth and seventh for the third. They will be referred to as Candidate A, B, and C, respectively.

If a lever is chosen as a candidate more than once, shift one lever to the right on the module, wrapping around the edges if necessary, until you land on a lever that has not been nominated yet.

Step 2 : The Elimination Round

Use each candidates' respective table to determine their scores.
All three levers will starts with 1 point.
If all there levers' score are unique, eliminate the one with the lowest score.
If there's a tie between two levers, eliminate the odd one out.
If all levers tie, take the last digit of the Serial number modulo 3, then, eliminate Candidate A if it's 0, B if it's 1, and C if it's 2.
