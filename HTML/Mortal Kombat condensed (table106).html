Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Mortal Kombat

On the Subject of Manageably Keeping Mortal Kombat

Test your might!

  • Using the table with your character's name in the top left corner, determine the moves, their order, and the fatality to be performed to solve the module.
  • The larger image represents your character, the smaller image is your enemy.
  • An incorrect press during the initial 3 attacks incurs a strike and resets successful attacks to 0. It may also change the enemy character.
  • An incorrect press during the fatality stage causes a strike without resetting anything.
