On the Subject of Simpletonge

Evil Simpleton says: DEFEAT.

Press any of the 64 buttons to start the module.

Each button will contain one of the eight characters from VICTORY!

Find eight buttons that contain each of the characters from VICTORY!, while also making sure that no two buttons can see each other vertically, horizontally, or diagonally, across the entire grid.

After finding the eight buttons, press them in the order that spells out VICTORY!

Pressing a button will turn it white. Pressing a button that does not spell out VICTORY! in the correct order or pressing a button that sees another button horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, will turn all buttons blue and reset your progress.
