On the Subject of Russian Dictation

Qu'est-ce qui se passe?

This module functions identically to Dictation, but it's russian. "Begin" is the top button, enter is on the right, etc.

After beginning, look for the first two words that appear (or their lengths) in the table below. You may begin again after the message finishes.

Go to the page described and look at its contents to figure out which word to input.

NOTE: if the word is written in Cyrillic/numbers, use your physical keyboard; if it's English/Latin, use the module's keyboard. The table has what to press on a standard QWERTY keyboard to type the word in Cyrillic.

Contact Possessed on discord for corrections or non-QWERTY support.
