On the Subject of Termet

flavour

First stage:

  • You will be on random cell of the table.
  • Directional buttons will move you in the corresponding direction by several cells. Table wraps around.
  • Do not go to the center. Going to the center in first stage will lead to strike, but you will stay in center.
  • When you are ready, you can press the screen to go to second stage.

Second stage:

  • You will be on the same starting cell of the table.
  • Directional buttons will work the same as in first stage.
  • To solve the module, go to the center in minimal possible amount of moves.
  • If you didn't move on second stage and pressed screen, module will reset. If you did move at least once, a strike will be given and module will reset. Also, if you press directional buttons too many times, a strike will be given and module will reset.

Resetting: when module resets, you come back to first stage but the starting position and directional offsets are going to be different.

Notice: Starting position cannot be in middle row or middle column. The minimal amount of moves for second stage are from 1 to 4 for one axis. Offsets of counter-directional buttons are coprime and their sum is never 9.
