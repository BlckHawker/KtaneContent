On the Subject of Wire Placement

Sometimes, the wires may look like a face, giving you a sense of calm... until you explode.

  • This module features a grid of wires.
  • There are always 8 wires present on the grid.
  • The wires can be red, blue, yellow, black, or white.
  • In the table below, use only the column corresponding to the color of the wire connected to C3.
  • Cut a wire if it matches a specific color and is connected to a designated spot on the grid, as indicated in the table.
  • Gray cells represent positions where the wire should never be cut because C3 is confirmed to not be that color.
