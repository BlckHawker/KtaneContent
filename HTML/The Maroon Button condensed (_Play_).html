On the Subject of The Maroon Button

Well, obviously this isn’t The Red Button! Are you colourblind?!

  • One of the flags, A, is from a different continent from the rest:
    • If the last digit of the serial number is even, press A first.
    • If the last digit of the serial number is odd, press A last.
  • Sort the four other flags based on the direction the screen is cycling in, then press them in the sorted order:
    • If the screen is cycling from left to right, order them from north to south.
    • If the screen is cycling from right to left, order them from south to north.
  • In the tables below, the flags are grouped by continent and are ordered from north to south within their groups (when read left to right).
