Below are the same sequences, but with every instance of some shape or color removed. Use the first digit of the serial number to get a shape to remove, and use the second digit to get a color to remove. Combine the two sequences to get a sequence of colored shapes by assigning the first color to the first shape, the second color to the second shape, and so on.

In each sequence on the module, remove all colored shapes that have the same shape that you removed or/and the same color that you removed. If the leftover sequence appears in the sequence of colored shapes obtained from the table above, press the yellow report button. Otherwise, press the green pass button.
Do this four times to solve the module.
