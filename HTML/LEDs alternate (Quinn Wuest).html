On the Subject of LEDs

For those who feel that buttons and levers and incandescent bulbs are just too complicated.

  • There will be 4 LEDs in a square formation. The LED pattern is taken from the table below. The pattern may be rotated.
  • Exactly one LED will be a different color from the diagram. This LED will never be the circled LED.
  • Press the different LED until its color is the correct color in the diagram. Then, press the circled LED.
  • Pressing any other LED will cause a strike.
