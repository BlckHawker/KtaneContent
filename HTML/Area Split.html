On the Subject of Area Split

The king grants you the largest plot of land for your good work as a lord. Unfortunately, he has no division skills.

Upon activation, four regions (assigned with four different colors) will fill up an 8×5 grid.

Select the region that covers the most area on the grid. The selected region will be checked after thirty seconds.

Failing to select a region in time or selecting the wrong region will incur a strike.
