On the Subject of Twisty Wire Hexaterminals

Give it a whirl. What could possibly go wrong?
Warning! May cause death by electrocution!

  1. Once the module is active, the terminal network is revealed and at least one pair of ports around its edges, an input and output, are highlighted in matching colours.
  2. Selecting a terminal rotates it, and all wires connected to it, 60° clockwise.
  3. All wires connected to an input are highlighted in its colour.
  4. To disarm the module, connect all inputs to their corresponding outputs through the network of wires before the timer reaches zero: the timer is extended for each pair of active ports.
  5. Under no circumstances may two inputs be connected to each other.
